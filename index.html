About

What is Kode with Klossy?

It is a program for young women to learn what coding is.

Applicants range from ages 13-18!

  • We have the best resources!
  • Teachers and TAs that have a passion to teach and the experience needed to make every kid inspired.


Who is our founder?

Karlie Kloss!

  • Karlie Kloss believes that education should be provided for every young women out there trying to pursue engineering.
